Philosophy

Wood Wellness started 2023 with a small furniture design collection. Passion for furniture and design was the starting point and the idea of ​​sustainability came naturally as we manufacture our products ourselves. Our aim is to be creative and contemporary in our design language. When it falls into place, the product can acquire a quality that makes it long-lasting also from a design perspective. We are inspired by Scandinavian design from the middle of the 20th century and that is something that is always present.
